How scoring works
1Finish a draft — that locks your roster.
2A scoring window opens — 1 day for NBA/MLB/NHL/UFC/Soccer, through end of tournament week for Golf (Thu–Sun), 7 days for NFL. Your players earn fantasy points from their real games.
3Stats come from live box scores, refreshed every few minutes — identical for everyone in the draft.
4Highest total when the window closes wins the squabble. 👑

How your account works
No password, no email. This device is your login — your profile, drafts, friends and chats are saved on SquabbleUP's servers under an ID this browser remembers.
⚠️ Clearing browser data or switching devices starts a fresh player, so don't clear it mid-squabble. Pass & play drafts live only on this device.
